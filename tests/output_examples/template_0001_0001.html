The Water Cycle: A Simple Guide

The water cycle, also known as the hydrologic cycle, shows how water moves on our planet. Water changes from one form to another and travels through the air, on land, and underground. This cycle is very important for life and affects weather.

1. **Water from oceans, lakes, and rivers** rises into the sky. This happens because the sun heats up the water, turning it into vapor. This part of the cycle is called evaporation.
